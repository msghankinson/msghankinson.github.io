Teaching

PSC 2211: State and Urban Politics

This course will introduce you to the politics of states and local government in the United States. We will begin by surveying the mechanics of these entities. Next, we will unpack what social science can tell us about who gets what and why in the state and local context. Finally, we will dive into specific policy areas, addressing current debates using the social science lens we've developed. Along the way, you will compile a political science policy paper on a topic of your choice.
